6. Elevation & Cards

Elevation Levels

Level 0 — Flat

No depth

No shadow, no border. Used directly on parchment canvas for inline content.

Level 1 — Contained

Border only

1px solid #f0eee6
Standard cards and sections.

Level 2 — Ring

Ring shadow

0 0 0 1px #d1cfc5
Interactive hover & focus states.

Level 3 — Whisper

Soft drop shadow

rgba(0,0,0,.05) 0 4px 24px
Elevated feature cards.

Card Variants

Standard

Feature Card

Ivory background, border-cream border, radius 8px, whisper shadow. The default content card.

Featured

Featured Card

Same as standard but radius 16px and 40px padding. For hero or pricing highlights.

Dark

Dark Card

Glassmorphism over black surface. Translucent white bg + backdrop-filter blur.
